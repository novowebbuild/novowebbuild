5 reasons your business
needs a website in 2025

📅 5 January 2025 · ⏱ 4 min read · ✍️ Novo Web Build
01

Even if your business is doing well on Instagram, a website is still one of the most powerful tools you can have. Here's why every small business needs one in 2025.

1. Google can find you

When someone searches for your service on Google, a restaurant, a gym, a café, Instagram doesn't appear in those results. A website with proper SEO does. That's potential customers you're currently missing every single day.

2. You own it completely

Instagram can change its algorithm, reduce your reach, or even ban your account. Your website belongs entirely to you. No algorithm, no platform risk, just a direct connection to your customers.

3. It gives you credibility

«Would you trust a business that only has Instagram over one with a professional website?»

A well-designed website instantly makes your business look more established and trustworthy. It's often the difference between someone calling you or moving on to a competitor.

4. It works for you 24/7

Your website is always live, always showing your services, always letting people book or contact you, even while you sleep. Instagram requires constant posting to stay visible.

5. It costs almost nothing to run

Hosting is completely free (via Netlify). Your only cost is the domain name, about €12 to 15 per year. That's less than €1.50 per month for a professional presence online.
